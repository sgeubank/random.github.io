40. IN COMMAND OF THE TRAINING CENTER FOR ARMY AND CORPS TROOPS AT CAMP JACKSON, S. C.

This was the Training Center for Army and Corps Special Troops, including Engineer, Medical, Ordnance, Signal Corps, and Quartermaster units, and I found the supervision of such varied training for overseas duty to be very interesting.

While at Camp Jackson I lived in temporary quarters and messed in the Bachelor's Mess.
